Accomodations - Room Discount

DOCS Education partners with hotels to provide attendees with reduced rates. Contact a DOCS representative for more information.

Talk to Your Team Members

The advantages of attending Oral Sedation Dentistry are all the more powerful when team members attend as well. Oral Sedation Dentistry has breakout groups for team members to learn how to implement sedation and further heighten its practice-wide benefits. Those who bring their team are more likely to implement and see an ROI.

Check Your State Regulations

Although most states require 16 to 25 CE credits to use minimal and moderate oral sedation, these regulations vary from state to state. Visit SedationRegulations.com to find out more about getting your permit application and meeting requirements. If in doubt, contact DOCS Education and be sure to speak with our Regulatory Counsel at the seminar. After the seminar, access to our Regulatory Counsel becomes an important membership benefit.

Multi-State Licensees

If you are registered in more than one state, please be sure to let us know, so that we can prepare accordingly. Different states have different requirements (i.e. the live patient requirements above).

Parking

Daily parking is free on Saturday and Sunday and is $25 daily for Friday in the parking garage located under the hotel. Overnight parking will be discounted to $15 per night for attendees staying at the hotel.

Attire

Casual attire is appropriate for the course. However, due to the sedative nature of the subject matter, we will keep the meeting space air conditioned so that people can stay awake and alert. We recommend that you wear warm clothes so that you can adjust to your own comfort level.

Shuttle

There is no shuttle from the airport.

Classes/Schedule

  • What time does class start?
    Class begins promptly at 7:30 a.m. each day, but plan on registering early (by 7:00 a.m.) if you want to avoid lines and enjoy breakfast.
  • What do I do if I have dietary restrictions?
    Please contact your CSR rep prior to the course at CSR@docseducation.com.
  • When do classes finish?
    On Friday and Saturday the end of the day is at 5:00 p.m.
    On Sunday the end of the day is at 1:00 p.m. for most courses. Pediatric Sedation Dentistry ends at 3:00 p.m. ACLS Online depends on overall attendance but usually ends at 4:00 p.m.
  • What is the Lunch & Learn about?
    On Friday the Internal/External Marketing Lunch & Learn will provide tips for sedation practice marketing.
    On Saturday the Eagleston Lunch & Learn is about financial security and employer security. It is mainly for doctors, and they are encouraged to attend!
  • What is Live Patient Experience?
    On Saturday we will present a live patient experience from the OSD main stage. While this is a requirement for California attendees seeking a sedation permit all are invited to attend for the extra hour of CE.
